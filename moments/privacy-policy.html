Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Information Collection and Use

Moments is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All tracking data, photos, and moments are stored locally on your device. You have complete control over your data.

Photos and Media

When you choose to add photos to your moments, these images are stored locally on your device. The app only accesses your photo library when you explicitly choose to add a photo.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for your moments
  • Photo Library: To select and save photos

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information is as secure as your device. We recommend using device-level security features like passcodes or biometric authentication.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
