Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Moments. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Moments stores all data locally on your device. We collect and store the following information:

  • Names you enter for relationships
  • Dates associated with your relationships
  • Photos you choose to add from your device

How We Use Your Information

All information you provide is used solely to display your relationship tracking data within the app. We do not:

  • Send your data to external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Analyze or process your personal data

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This means your information never leaves your device and is accessible only to you. If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be permanently deleted.

Photos and Media

When you add photos to your relationships, these images are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, copy, or transmit your photos to any external servers or services.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can:

  • View all stored information within the app
  • Edit or delete any relationship at any time
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated last updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
