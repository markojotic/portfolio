Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our conversation starter app ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection and Storage

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know:

  • Saved Questions: When you save questions within the app, this data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your saved questions.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share with third parties.

Local Data Storage

All data saved within the app (such as bookmarked questions) is stored locally on your device using the device's local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not synced to any cloud service or external server operated by us.

Device Permissions

Our app does not request access to your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, or any other device features.

Data Deletion

You can delete your saved questions at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data from your device.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services in our app. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
